about us

{about san antonio coding challenge}

The San Antonio Coding Challenge, which started in January 2016, is a monthly networking event featuring a coding competition and other fun games. The event is put together by volunteers from the San Antonio Coding Challenge Meetup group (previously known as The San Antonio PHP Meetup group) and paid for by the generous donations of our sponsors. The monthly Coding Challenge is free and open for everyone.
